THE INVENTOR'S TRUE
STORY

Curiously, it all started with a phone call.

I had not been involved in, or aware of, my wife's problems in the bathroom. Like most of us, this was personal and not discussed.

Ring, ring! There was no phone in the bathroom, so I became her personal secretary, answering and bringing the phone to her.

Soon after, I installed a phone in the bathroom so I could renounce my secretarial duties. However, I now knew that she spent a lot of valuable time in the bathroom serving her needs.

I then added a TV so she could have something to do to prevent boredom. (What can I say, I'm a wonderful husband!)

These changes helped, but it was not until I heard on the "Shark Tank" program about a potty assist leg elevation device that there seemed to be a solution.

I bought the product, and it helped save my wife time in the bathroom. However, now we also had two new problems.

First, I am about 5'10" tall, and the "one size fits all" solution was uncomfortable and useless to a person of my height.

Second, the constant presence of this gadget around the base of our toilet was bulky and in the way whether sitting or standing to use the toilet. As a standing user, I found myself having to maneuver around the potty assist several times a day to serve my normal needs. And other times, I would hit my foot against it while carefully aiming, and even in the seated position, the wide, bulky part bumped into my calves and obstructed my own comfort zone.

The competitor's device was also very conspicuous with no streamlined appearance for our luxurious bathroom. Simply put, it just wasn't sexy.

I am an engineer with many patents to my name, and I have been solving problems my entire life. My instincts kicked into gear, and I imagined a better solution.

This led to the inspiration of Ultra EZ Potty. All of these problems were economically solved in a sleek, sturdy, height-adjustable product that accommodates all users!

How easy? Ultra EZ!
